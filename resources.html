Case studies from the floor

Real situations, real numbers — the short version of how each result happened.

🚚

97% on-time, 35 vendors

Situation: five brands, ~3M pieces/year, capacity scattered across 35 suppliers.
Action: tightened capacity monitoring, weekly vendor follow-ups, early risk flags.
Result: 97% on-time delivery in the latest season — while order volume grew 10%.

🌱

30% sustainable at Celio

Situation: brand target to grow certified sustainable production.
Action: vendor OCS/recycled programs plus an OEKO-TEX STeP certification push toward 80% vendor coverage.
Result: 30% of the collection in sustainable production, on the road to the MADE IN GREEN label.

📈

USD 35M from a standing start

Situation: New Wave Group wanted a trading business in Bangladesh. Headcount: two merchandisers.
Action: built buyer relationships, quoting, and execution from zero — 15 accounts opened.
Result: USD 35 million in annual order value by 2018.
