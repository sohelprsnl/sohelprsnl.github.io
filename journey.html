Chapter 3 · Dec 2018 – Feb 2024

Owning the account

Account Lead Merchandiser, Celio Sourcing Limited, Dhaka
CelioVisit website ↗

Five years independently running the French retailer’s tee, polo, sweatshirt, nightwear, pyjama, and accessory accounts, 1.5 million pieces a year, 70+ styles a season, 14 factories, six buying seasons annually. This is where account ownership became second nature.

  • 94% on-time delivery, holding suppliers to the T&A from lab dip to final inspection.
  • Helped lift sustainable production to 30% of the collection (organic OCS, recycled); drove vendor OEKO-TEX STeP certification toward the MADE IN GREEN label.
  • Executed licensed-product programs across major IPs, Marvel, Warner Bros. (Batman, Superman, Harry Potter, Rick & Morty), Cartoon Network, Star Wars, Naruto, Dragon Ball, One Piece, Pokémon, and NBA, handling artwork approvals and licensor compliance.
  • Yarn and fabric price tracking behind every negotiation.

Chapter 2 · Dec 2014 – Nov 2018

Building a business

Key Lead Merchandiser (Senior Role), New Wave Group, Dhaka
New Wave GroupVisit website ↗ Multi Sourcing Asia Ltd.Visit website ↗

A year on New Wave’s own brands (Craft, Cottover), then the leap: two merchandisers starting a trading operation from zero through the group’s intercompany, Multi Sourcing Asia Ltd. By the time I left, it ran USD 35 million in annual order value.

  • 15 buyer accounts opened, Björn Borg, SEBAGO, Salming Sports, Volvo AB, Ralawise, Aditya Birla Fashion & Retail, and more.
  • Negotiated both directions, buyers and suppliers, protecting margin while staying competitive.
  • End-to-end: development presentations, T&A plans, approvals, QA files, LC coordination.
