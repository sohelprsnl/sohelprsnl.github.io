Chapter 3 · Dec 2018 – Feb 2024

Owning the account

Account Lead Merchandiser — Celio Sourcing Limited, Dhaka
CelioVisit website ↗

Five years independently running the French retailer’s tee, polo, sweatshirt, nightwear, pyjama, and accessory accounts — 1.5 million pieces a year, 70+ styles a season, 14 factories, six buying seasons annually. Managed the complete product lifecycle from development through shipment, including costing, negotiations, quality control, and sustainability initiatives.

  • 94% on-time delivery, holding suppliers to the T&A from lab dip to final inspection.
  • Lifted sustainable production to 30% of the collection (organic OCS, recycled materials); pushed vendor OEKO-TEX STeP certification toward 80% coverage for the MADE IN GREEN label.
  • Updated all sustainability data in the MADE IN GREEN global system to enable end-customer traceability.
  • Executed licensed-product programs across major entertainment IPs — Marvel, Warner Bros. (Batman, Superman, Harry Potter, Rick & Morty), Cartoon Network, Star Wars, Naruto, Dragon Ball, One Piece, Pokémon, and NBA — handling artwork approvals and licensor compliance.
  • Tracked yarn and fabric prices to build cost data behind every negotiation; managed development sample lead times (7–14 days for local fabrics, 3–4 weeks for imported).
  • Provided technical guidelines to suppliers; arranged and monitored all sampling activities (styling, PP, size sets, final approvals).

Chapter 2 · Dec 2014 – Nov 2018

Building a business

Chapter 1 · Nov 2011 – Dec 2014

The floor

Merchandiser / Assistant Merchandiser — Crystal Martin Apparel (Marks & Spencer), Mymensingh
Crystal GroupVisit website ↗

Factory-side merchandising for Marks & Spencer (M&S), managing circular knit and woven production programs from order collection to shipment. Costings, TNA plans, PP meetings, daily production reports — with the production floor right downstairs. Everything since stands on this foundation.

  • Prepared initial costings for buyer inquiries; built critical-path and T&A plans with IE and planning departments to ensure on-time delivery.
  • Arranged Pre-Production meetings with approved samples and trims cards; highlighted critical areas with technical, production, and quality teams.
  • Monitored all sampling activities: lab dips, strike-offs, embellishments, PP samples, and final approvals per M&S specifications.
  • Managed fabric, trims, and accessories inventory through ERP system; tracked in-house materials and coordinated with factory management on shortages.
  • Oversaw cutting, print/embroidery, washing, finishing — monitored daily and tracked physical/chemical test reports.
  • Coordinated final inspections with AQL teams; ensured quality execution through to shipment.
