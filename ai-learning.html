AI learning journey

Learning AI the way I learned merchandising: by doing.

Structured coursework, daily tool use in live sourcing work, and a public log of what holds up — and what doesn’t.

Why this page exists

Most of RMG hasn’t met AI yet. I’m making sure I have.

Garment manufacturing runs on experience, relationships, and spreadsheets. All three are about to change shape. I’m not waiting to be told how — I’m studying it, applying it in live merchandising work, and writing about what holds up. This page is the public log.

“An AI tool that saves a merchandiser two hours a day is worth more than a keynote about the future of fashion.”

Applied today

How I already use AI in merchandising work

📊

Reporting & analysis

ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini run through the daily workflow — weekly reports, vendor data consolidation, supplier-performance and capacity analysis, production follow-up updates, presentation (PPT) preparation, and meeting prep — alongside Power BI and advanced Excel.

✉️

Business communication

Faster, cleaner correspondence with Poland HQ and 35 vendors — AI-drafted, human-judged. The judgment is the job; AI just removes the typing.

📝

Writing & publishing

Research support for the weekly newsletter and daily posts — AI helps gather; fourteen years on the floor decides what’s true.
